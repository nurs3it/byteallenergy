About ByteAll Energy

Digital Oilfield, IAM, and production modelling specialists

Our Story

Since 2017 we have helped operators across Central Asia and Europe strengthen production forecasting, automate surveillance, and modernise digital oilfield operations with bespoke engineering and software teams.

Mission

To empower the petroleum industry with innovative digital solutions and expertise, driving efficiency, optimising performance, and shaping the future of energy.

Vision

To be a global leader in digital oilfield technologies, fostering a smarter, more efficient, and sustainable energy sector.

Since our founding in 2017, we have been at the forefront of digital transformation in the oil & gas industry. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between traditional engineering practices and modern digital solutions.

Integrated production modelling and network optimisation
PVT characterisation, transformation, and reservoir modelling
Well and reservoir modelling with nodal analysis and multiple-rate calculations
